Tomato Fried Eggs

Description

This is my favorite hometown dish, that is easy to make and is a great combination with rice.

Ingredients
  • Tomatoes x 2
  • Eggs x 3
  • Sesame Oil x 2 teaspoon
  • Sauce:
    • Ketchup x 4 tablespoon
    • Cornstarch x 15g
    • White granulated sugar x 2 teaspoon
    • Ginger x 30g
    • Salt x 1 teaspoon
    • Cold water x 80g

Steps

  1. Mix the sauce as listed in ingredients
  2. Beat the eggs and add sesame oil
  3. Bring up a pan, heat it with oil, and scramble the eggs until they are in shape but still moist
  4. Remove the eggs, stir fry sping onions with oil
  5. Add tomatoes with water and let them cook for 2-3 minutes
  6. Add the sauce
  7. Mix in the eggs and stir until desired thickness of the sauce
