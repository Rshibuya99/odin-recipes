Pancakes

Description

Who doesn't like a classic breakfast choice, pancakes!

Ingredients
  • All Purpose Flour x 1.5 cups
  • Baking Powder x 3.5 teaspoons
  • White Sugar x 1 teaspoon
  • Salt x 0.25 teaspoon
  • Milk x 1.25 cups
  • Melted butter x 3 tablespoons
  • Egg x 1
Steps
  1. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar and salt together in a large bowl
  2. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter and egg; mix until smooth
  3. Lightly oil a pan and heat it up
  4. Pour the batter into the pan, using 1/4 cup for each pancake
  5. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry for about 2-3 minutes
  6. Flip and cook until brown on the other side
  7. Repeat with the rest of the batter
